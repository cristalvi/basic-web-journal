The Beginning
September 20, 2017 / by Cristal Fernett

So, I think I need to make an adjustment in my life. I don't want to lose sight of who I am, and right now I feel as lost as ever. Every time I go about being alone, but then have the opportunity to make a connection with someone, I freak out.

I push and push and drive myself crazy. I so want every opportunity to BE THE ONE that I get so disappointed when things don't go perfectly. Then I make some sort of stupid little mistake that almost ruins everything.
