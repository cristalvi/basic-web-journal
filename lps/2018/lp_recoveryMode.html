Recovery Mode

February 26, 2018 / by Cristal Fernett

This week has been hard. I haven't been working a whole lot because I got my taxes back, and that has left time for me to play a lot. On two occassions I let loose with a few of my friends from work, as well as my poker people. Both nights resulted in bad situations for me to be in. No matter how old I get, you can't escape stupid.

The first night was a drunken bar-to-hotel hop. I wanted to get to know the people from work. It was very stupid because I drove home very drunk. Not smart. The second night was worse I think. I got just as drunk and drove again. What is wrong with me?

I had been talking to Evan, trying to get him to hang out again, and for a minute there was a plan. I knew he would cancel, which he did, so I preceeded to get trashed and gamble up. I lost two grand that night. Stupid. That's one thing I've learned...never gamble on a broken heart.

The worst part about all of that is that I think I killed any shot at getting to know Evan. Stupid me sent stupid text messages because I was hurt and emotional, on top of being drunk off my ass. He hasn't texted me back since. It's really bad because I really liked him. There aren't a whole lot of guys that check most boxes. He was one. I'm not sure I can forgive myself for my choices that night.

On a different note, I did text Michael to open the door for communication, and he messaged me right back. We had a nice little exchange. It was very pleasant and friendly. I couldn't have asked for more...or better. Though, I'm not sure what I want from him. I'm lonely again so I can't trust myself.

I can only hope. I have been hoping for so long for something to come along. I see people living and sharing company and it just kills me. I know I'm not perfect, but come on! I'm a nice person with a big heart and no one to share it with. Shouldn't that be a tradgedy?
