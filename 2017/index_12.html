Let's End This

Alone

December 8, 2017 / by Cristal Fernett

I have been doing a lot of thinking and have come to a realization. I will never get the relationship that I am looking for if I keep going along accepting substandard behavior from guys. I have to speak my mind and then move on when they choose something other than what I can offer them. It is in my best interests and if I am alone for the rest of my life, that will be ok.

Anyone that cannot make me a priority in their life does not deserve to have any piece of my heart. They do not deserve to be a priority in my life. They would never fully appreciate the person that I am, nor would they know how to be the person that I want in my life. I have never been a priority for anyone, and I deserve to be the number one priority for someone...for the one.
