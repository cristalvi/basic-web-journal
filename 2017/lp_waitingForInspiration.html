Waiting For Inspiration

October 10, 2017 / by Cristal Fernett

Today is tough. First of all I woke up late again! I even went to bed early. Figures. Second, I never really heard from Frank yesterday until he went to bed. That is not the relationship I want with someone who wants to be with me.

On one hand, I deserve that for what I did to him when I met Michael. On the other, I need more connection than that. Not speaking throughout the day makes me feel very alone, and I have never responded well to feeling alone. I don't know wheather or not to give him time. I don't want a repeat of what happened last time, because I'm not going to wait for anyone when I don't have a reason to. At any moment, opportunities come and go, and missed opportunities are the worst waste of time; that is the only truth that I know.

I feel like I'm spinning in circles again. Something is missing in my life and I don't know what that is. There are times when I feel most like me, and that's usually when I am alone. Why is that? Am I supposed to be all alone? Am I here just to raise my boys and that's it? I can't believe that God would give me the power to love this much and not put anyone here on earth to share my life with. I can't believe that. I won't believe that.

Article By Cristal Fernett

Born and raised in Washington, the author discusses life from the homegrown perspective of a unique graphic designer.
