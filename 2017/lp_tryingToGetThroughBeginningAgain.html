Trying to Get Through Beginning Again

October 6, 2017 / by Cristal Fernett

It's been harder than I thought trying to get Michael out of my head. I was fine for a day or two, but I've been gambling again. That might be a problem for me. I seem to self destruct when I play to pass the night away. I need a distraction, but this isn't working.

It started coming back to me in waves, all the memories. The feelings I had for him are not gone like I thought they were. I believe in my heart that he is the one for me, but he doesn't want me like I need him to. He was my perfect and I am not his. That is a very tough thing to come to grips with. I miss him. I miss his kid. I miss the feelings I had when I was with him. He made me want to be better. I was better.

How do I date someone else when he is the one I want? It doesn't feel right. It feels wrong to go out with anyone who is not him. Frank wants to see me. I don't feel what I felt before with Frank. I think it's because he hurt me before, so now it's nearly impossible to trust him again. I forgive him, but I can't trust him. I'm so screwed up in the head, I don't think I can trust anyone.
