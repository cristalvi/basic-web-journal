A New World
September 21, 2019 / by Cristal Fernett

This is the first post I've done in a long while. It's true, I've been busy, but life happens. I should mention that life has been pretty great for a while. As it so happens, I was busy doing the usual rounds of meeting people when, for some reason, I decided to go out with somebody I was talking to briefly. The plan was to have a few drinks and see if this was someone who I wanted to get to know.

That night led to many others. We got along extremely well, and before you know it, we were crazy for each other. It's just one of those crazy things that happen in life. He has become my best friend and greatest lover.
