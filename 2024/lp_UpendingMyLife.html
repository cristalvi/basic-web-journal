Upending My Life

Saturday 24, 2024 / by Cristal Fernett

Yesterday was the end of Me & Dylan. I cannot say how hurt I was after coming home from work. Dylan had been st it agin, 10 cans deep into a case (not to mention the last of my Tito bottle). After begging him to stop drinking and work on himself and his situation, he promised again that he would try.

How can he blatently ignore the ordeal he has put us in. He does not care to change things. He does not care if he loses me. So...I went up to him for a conversation. The whole thing was short and sweet. I said I think we should separate, and he said OK. That was it, ok. After five years, that was all the response he gave me. I could not believe his lack of response to me. I will never forget it.
