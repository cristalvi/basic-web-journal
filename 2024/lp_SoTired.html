So Tired.

Sunday 25, 2024 / by Cristal Fernett

I have not slept well the last few nights. Starting with being stupid drunk again, falling asleep at the poker table and blowing all of my money, and ending with crying on the couch all freaking day and night long. I hate myself more than I ever think I have. I don't think that's a good sign of things to come.

It's become very clear that no one values me at all. There is not one person who is willing to show me companionship. I am not worthy of hanging out with, as far as everything goes. There is no value in me. That's the only message I am getting from everyone in my life. There is no good reason why, that's just the only thing that keeps happening to me.

I don't know what to do. There is something wrong with me, but I don't know what it is. I have tried so many different things over so many different years, and nothing has ever changed or gotten better. I am always and forever alone. I will end up being that lonely, sad old woman who's too attached to her pets and scaring little children. I will one day die alone and scared, and no one will miss me. There will be no one there to help me when I need someone. I really think I will go crazy, literally fucking insane.
