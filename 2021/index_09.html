End or Beginning

September 26, 2021 / by Cristal Fernett

The last week or so has been extremely hard for me. Thursday night was bad, I mean really bad, as far as things had been going for me the past few years. Out of nowhere, Dylan explodes his feelings all over me, and by the end of a long night, we're screaming at each other.

The next day I spent crying at work, like an idiot. When I got home, it's been pretty much how I figured it would be. Silent. It's Sunday now, and we are supposed to talk. I tried the night before, but he was already asleep. I don't know what to say to him.
