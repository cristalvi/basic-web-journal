Where It All Started

Another Beginning
September 25, 2017 / by Cristal Fernett

This morning was very upsetting. The night before, I had asked Michael to call me so that we could talk, and he did. We had a very awkward conversation, where I told him what was bugging me and where he avoided talking to me. He said he had to get off the phone to take Nathan's temperature, and then 'click'...he was gone.

He also mentioned that he was going through some stuff, but was not telling me anything. He said he was not really talking to anyone else either, and did not really go into anything more.

I DO NOT understand. It makes no sense to me what happened between us. Is he mad because I did not text him for a day? Is he truly blowing me off because he IS that much of an asshole? Is he really busy, sick, off his normal behavior, and I should just let him recover himself? All I know is that I am hurting on the inside. I am hurting and he does not seem to care.

I don't understand what he wants from me or what he is trying to accomplish. I'm not sure how to be with him. I don't know what to do other than be me, honest and truthful. I don't know whether or not to be happy, or angry, or scared, or feel nothing, or something else.

I know I am chasing my tail now. I keep looking for something that I want in a place where I don't think it can be. The love that I want, and the comfort and safety that I crave, I don't think I will ever find in a man. I think I need to keep God in my mind and in my heart, especially if I want to feel those things. I need to be the best me I can be for myself, not for anyone or anything else.

Trying to Remain Centered and Focused
September 22, 2017 / by Cristal Fernett

Through all of the emotional uphieval, I am trying to find my roots again. I want to be someone stronger and more grounded in who I really am. I'm taking small steps at a time but I feel like the loneliest person in the city.

Michael won't talk to me. It doesn't make much sense to me. Ever since he got back we haven't spent much time together, even talking. He got sick and now only texts me after I text first. When he does, I get short and sweet messages. It's absolutely frustrating. I can't tell how he feels.

The Beginning
September 20, 2017 / by Cristal Fernett

So, I think I need to make an adjustment in my life. I don't want to lose sight of who I am, and right now I feel as lost as ever. Every time I go about being alone, but then have the opportunity to make a connection with someone, I freak out.

I push and push and drive myself crazy. I so want every opportunity to BE THE ONE that I get so disappointed when things don't go perfectly. Then I make some sort of stupid little mistake that almost ruins everything.
