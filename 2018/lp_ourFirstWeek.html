Our First Week

March 5, 2018 / by Cristal Fernett

Dustin and I had a great time talking over coffee. I was so nervous, but it went well. There was something about him. We decided to go on a second date on Friday. I was not sure what to expect. I think I was more nervous for that date than our first meeting.

Our first date was at Bahama Breeze in Southcenter. He actually picked me up and drove, which was nice. I wasn't sure how that would go. It was pretty busy when we got there, but luckily we got a couple of seats at the bar. He was pretty intent on making this a 'buy a bottle of wine' kind of event. So we drank and talked. It was really nice.

When he told me about his idea to drive to his buddy's house, I wasn't sure what to think. Did he want a second opinion...you know...a friend's approval? Plus it was so far away. The drive went by quick enough. We did have to stop and pick up another bottle of wine and a corkscrew, though. His friend was nice enough. Good guy. Kind of goofy, but good guy. It was nice to gradually get closer to Dustin. We played pool and had some laughs. All in all, a good time.

Afterwards, he came back to my place where...I guess you could say...I had my way with him! OH MY GOD was it fun, and good! I swear he is so perfect for me. How is that possible? It seams like we immediately just fell into this grove. It's wonderfull! Not to mention the next day, I was on my lunch break, thinking I was seeing his car everywhere, when he texted me. I had a feeling he was doing something...and wouldn't you know it, he came to my work to surprise me! He stayed for a few AND bought me a rose! In one fell swoop, he did the two things that I have been wanting someone to do for me for over six years. He's perfect. That's all there is to it.

I really hope he is the one for me. I feel like myself around him. I am totally crazy about him right now. He seems like a nice person and I don't think he would hurt me like the others...but what do I know. I also thought that about ALL of the others too. Maybe he is the right one. We will see.
